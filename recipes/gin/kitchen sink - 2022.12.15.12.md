# Gin

## Kitchen sink gin (15 December 2022)

### Recipe

  - 1L of 55% neutral spirits
  - 18g juniper berries
  - 9g coriander seeds
  - 5g rose petals
  - 2g Angelica root
  - 1g hibiscus flower
  - 1g rose hip
  - 0.4g grains of paradise
  - 0.35g burdock root
  - 0.2g orris root
  - 1 cardamom pod
  - peel of 1/2 orange
